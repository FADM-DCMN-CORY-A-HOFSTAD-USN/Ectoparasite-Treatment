Clinical Readme: Ectoparasite Treatment Protocols
=================================================

Overview
--------

This repository contains clinical treatment protocols for ectoparasites, developed through collaboration between Virus Treatment Centers (VirusTC) and the Agriculture Pathology Institute. These guidelines provide standardized prescriptions for managing organisms such as ticks, fleas, lice, and mites that burrow into or attach to the skin for extended periods.

Clinical Brand: Virus Treatment Centers (VirusTC)
-------------------------------------------------

Virus Treatment Centers provides specialized clinical content and pharmaceutical-grade solutions for patient care.

### Approved Clinical Prescriptions

The following VirusTC-branded treatments are approved for clinical use:

-   **Aromatodis 100μl**: Administer three (3) droplets per day with meals.

-   **Ledama 200c**: Administer five (5) tablets per day with meals.

-   **Ectogano Solution 2oz**: One (1) serving per day.

-   **Ectogano Tablet 140mg**: Two (2) tablets daily (at wake and before sleep) for a duration of six (6) months.

For more information, visit <https://virustreatmentcenters.com>.
